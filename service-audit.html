SERVICE AUDIT / MYSTERY CONSUMER

An incognito visit to a physical store by trained auditors to evaluate the overall staff performance. 

HOW IT IS DONE:

  1. Definition of the specific areas of focus and items to be checked.
  2. Design of the questionnaire and items for review.
  3. Training and preparation of auditors/mystery shoppers to conduct service audits.
  4. Auditors to conduct incognito service audits in the stores and fill in the questionnaire with detailed comments.
  5. Validation, analysis and checking of results.
  6. Submission of qualitative and quantitative reports with annecdotal and audio evidence. 

WHAT YOU WILL GET:

  1. A quantitative and qualitative report that summarizes the service  results.
  2. Individual audit results which include the questionnaire and audio evidence taken by auditors in store.
